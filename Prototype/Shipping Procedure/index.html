Shipping Procedure

Steps to Send a Package:

  • 1. Prepare Your Package: Pack your item in a suitable box or envelope. Make sure the item is well-protected.
  • 2. Measure and Weigh: Measure the dimensions of the package and weigh it. This information will be required to calculate the shipping cost.
  • 3. Label Your Package: Clearly write or print the address of the recipient. Make sure the label is secure and clearly visible.
  • 4. Choose a Shipping Service: Visit our website or one of our offices to choose a shipping service that suits your needs.
  • 5. Pay for the Shipping: You can pay for the shipping service online or at our office.
  • 6. Drop Off or Schedule a Pickup: Drop off your package at our office or schedule a pickup at your location.
  • 7. Track Your Package: You can track the progress of your shipment using the tracking number provided.
